Privacy Policy

Toolcrib - Equipment Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or device identifiers.

The only network communication the app makes is to Apple's App Store servers, solely to process in-app purchases via StoreKit. No other data leaves your device.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it.
